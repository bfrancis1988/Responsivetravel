Italy is the fifth most visisted country in the wold. With its rich history and splendid food it is easy to see why. There is plenty to see, do, and eat. They are also home to some of the most natural sights to see. They are home to Europes only three active volcanoes and the must see Blue Grotto. Whether you want to visit the birth place of the pizza in naples or to one of the 1500 lakes you can always find something do.
